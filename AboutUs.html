about us

Your Journey to a Healthier Smile Begins Here

Founded in 1991 by Dr. Chandrasekhar, our clinic has been a pillar of excellence in dental care for over three decades. With specialists in Orthodontics, Endodontics, Oral Surgery, Prosthodontics, Pedodontics, and Periodontics , we offer cutting-edge treatments tailored to every dental need.

What truly sets us apart is our legacy of expertise and trust—thousands of patients, decades of experience, and an unwavering commitment to exceptional care. A year ago, we elevated our services by moving into a state-of-the-art facility, featuring the latest advancements in dental technology to provide precision, comfort, and outstanding results.

Our clinic aims to provide friendly and caring dental services. We offer treatments like root canals, crowns and bridges, implants, extractions, Invisalign, braces, pediatric and geriatric care, along with advanced laser dentistry.
